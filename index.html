Biography

I am a 26 - year old Academic and Researcher Currently serving as a Visiting Faculty (Part-Time) at the Centre for Innovation & Entrepreneurship (CIE), Jamia Millia Islamia University, New Delhi. In this role, I teach Python Programming and Data Analytics, while Mentoring students in Innovation, Entrepreneurship, and Industry-Oriented Technical Skills. I teach 6 Batches of Bachelor’s and Master’s students from both Technical and Non-Technical backgrounds, guiding them in Complete Dissertation/ Project Development based on Real-world problem-solving. I bring over 2 + years of Teaching and mentoring experience. Alongside this, I am completing my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) and working as a Teaching Assistant and Researcher at Amity University, Noida under the Supervision of Prof.Dr. Rajbala Semon, Contributing to Research in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. I also have Teaching Assistant (TA) and Research Assistant (RA) Experience Mentoring 7+ students , where I guide them in preparing Dissertations, Research Papers, and Academic Projects.

My areas of Interest include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Computer Vision. I have worked on Multiple Real-world projects Involving Neuro-Symbolic Systems, Object Detection, Image Processing, Predictive Analytics, and GenAI, focusing on building practical and impactful AI-driven solutions. I also gained industry experience as a Data Scientist (Trainee) at S.O. Infotech Pvt. Ltd. Noida, where I worked on Real-world Problems using Real Datasets, Applying Machine Learning, Data analysis, and AI Techniques to develop practical solutions.

I have completed my Associate, Bachelor’s, and Master’s degrees from some of India’s Top Universities, Establishing a strong and diverse academic foundation. I hold a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) from Amity University, Noida, and a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Lovely Professional University. I also earned an Associate Degree/Diploma in Computer Engineering (3 years) from Jamia Millia Islamia University, along with a Technical Certification (2 years) in the Electrician trade from the National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT) Industrial Training Institute (ITI), reflecting my multidisciplinary and technically rigorous academic background.

I have Authored and Presented Multiple Research Papers at National and International Platforms. My work Includes a Publication indexed in IEEE Xplore and presentations at IIT Mandi, NIT Uttarakhand, and Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra. I am Currently working on 6+ Research papers in Collaboration with International Professors from the United States and other Countries. My Ongoing Research Focuses on Neuro-Symbolic AI, Real-Time Object Detection, and Video Super-Resolution, with several papers under review with Wiley, Springer Nature, and Elsevier. I have gained International Exposure through Fully Funded Programs at the Hertie School Data Science Lab (Berlin) and McMedHacks Lab (Canada), where I worked on Data Science and Medical AI Applications. I am a recipient of the Fully Funded AWS Machine Learning Scholarship and have Earned over 30+ Certifications in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Image Processing.

I have also received Multiple International Opportunities for Higher Education, Including Offers from Universities in the United States, UK and other Countries, with Scholarships of up to $50,000. These include The University of Rochester, The University of Sheffield, The University of Bath, Northeastern University, London Campus, and other (10+) leading institutions for advanced studies in Computer Science.

I actively participate in National and International initiatives related to Technology and Innovation. I have Contributed to UNESCO’s IndiaAI stakeholder consultation and attended the India Space Congress 2025. Additionally, I have Completed the NEP 2020 Orientation and Sensitization Programme under the Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme (MMTTC) of the University Grants Commission (UGC), India Organized by the Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Centre (MMTTC), Jamia Millia Islamia – AI-Focused Refresher Course. I have also Participated in AI-focused academic refresher programs Aimed at Strengthening Interdisciplinary Research and Teaching in Artificial Intelligence.
